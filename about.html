    • About Us

    Boss Airlines is one of the most preferred airline by the passengers of India. The airline offers one of the most luxurious and superior flight rides at the most affordable rates. Since our inception in 2018, we have grown from a carrier with one plane to a fleet of 100 passenger flights today. A uniform fleet for each type of operation, high operational reliability make us one of the most reliable airlines in India. We currently operate flights to 4 destinations – Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi and Kolkata.


    The Preferred Airline

    We are constantly enhancing our engagement with our passengers to augment their travel experience. From multichannel direct sales (including online flight booking, call centers and airport counters), to online flight status checking, an exclusive Boss Airlines app for Android(Under development), we have transformed air travel in India. Today, we are India’s most preferred airline. At Boss Airlines, low fares come with high quality.


    Great Place to Work

    Being courteous and hassle free starts with being a hassle-free place to work. A highly engaged and motivated workforce leads to higher levels of customer service. This training facility is considered to be one the best aviation training facilities in India. With our people-friendly culture at the heart of all we do, we continuously help the company staff find work-life balance.


    Social Initiative

    Our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative focuses on three broad themes: Children and Education, Women Empowerment and Environment. We work towards upliftment of communities not just around us but also far-flung areas in the country. After all, India’s holistic progress is rooted in the collective aspirations of its people.
